About Me

Hello! I'm Ryan Byers, a passionate Computer Science graduate with a focus on web development. Right now, I am currently pursuing a position as a full-stack web developer! If you are hiring, please view my Contact section for details on how to get in touch with me.

My interest in programming began in my first programming course in Wor-Wic Community College in Salisbury, Maryland back in 2019. I found the process of writing code and building software to be incredibly fulfilling, but I still wasn't sure I wanted to dedicate my career to it, so I made the decision to wait on picking CS as my major. I instead dedicated my time towards an Associates in General Studies, which I was awarded in May 2021.

From here, my path forward began to become clearer to me. After graduating from Wor-Wic in 2021, my family and I moved to Missouri in order to live closer to my older brother. With this move, I was forced to put my education on hold in order to become eligible for in-state tuition. During this time, I got a job as a bellperson at a luxury hotel located in downtown Kansas City, Loews Hotel. At Loews is where I learned how to work alongside people within the corporate world, and also how to work effectively as a member of a larger team in order to handle team-sized tasks such as room drop deliveries, VIP arrivals, and luggage storage. My job at Loews taught me invaluable interpersonal communication skills and exposed me to a variety of people and ideas from all across the world. After a year had past, I was finally officially considered a permanent resident of Missouri, and thus I could return to school. From 2021-2025, I worked part-time at Loews, as a bellman whilst I pursued my Bachelor's Degree full-time at The University of Missouri-Kansas City (UMKC).

Having just recently graduated from UMKC in the Spring of 2025, this brings the story to current day: where I am currently pursuing a full-time position as a web developer!
